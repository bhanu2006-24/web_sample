Bhanu Pratap Saini

Based In

Jaipur, India

Specialty

Editorial, Fashion, Portrait

"Photography is the pause button of life."

My journey began with a simple curiosity for light and shadow, evolving into a passionate pursuit of visual storytelling. I believe that every face tells a story, and every landscape holds a secret waiting to be whispered.

Krishna Lens was founded on the principle that photography should be an immersive experience. We don't just capture how things look; we capture how they feel. From the chaotic energy of urban streets to the silent intimacy of a studio portrait, our work is defined by a cinematic quality that transcends the ordinary.
