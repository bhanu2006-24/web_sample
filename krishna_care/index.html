Our Mission

Why We Started

Krishna Care was born in 2018 from a simple observation: while resources exist, they often don't reach those who need them most. What started as a weekend food drive by students in Jaipur has grown into a structured organization helping thousands.

Vision

A world where dignity is a right, not a privilege.

Goal

To impact 100,000 lives by 2026.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about donating and volunteering.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations made to Krishna Care are eligible for 50% tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.

Can I sponsor a specific child?

Yes! Through our 'Project Vidya', you can sponsor a child's education for a year. You will receive quarterly progress reports.

How do I volunteer?

Simply fill out the form on our Contact page or DM us on Instagram. We have weekend volunteering slots available in Jaipur.
