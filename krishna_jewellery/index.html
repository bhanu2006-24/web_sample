The Story of Aurum

Founded in 1985 by master goldsmith Marco Arge in the heart of Geneva, Switzerland, **Aurum Fine Jewellery** was born from a singular vision: to blend timeless European artistry with ethically sourced, breathtaking materials. Marco believed that jewellery is not merely an accessory, but a wearable legacy—a piece of history to be cherished and passed down through generations.

Our atelier upholds a tradition of meticulous craftsmanship. Every diamond is hand-selected and certified by GIA standards, every setting is polished to perfection using proprietary techniques, and every design respects the natural beauty of the gemstone. We specialize in bespoke creations and signature collections that embody classic elegance and modern flair.

At Aurum, **sustainability** is paramount. We are committed to responsible sourcing, ensuring that every stone we use is conflict-free and traceable. When you choose Aurum, you invest in a piece that is beautiful inside and out, supporting a future where luxury and ethics coexist seamlessly.

"Creating beauty that endures for a lifetime."

Complimentary Shipping & Delivery

We offer complimentary, fully insured, worldwide shipping on all orders. Due to the handcrafted nature of our pieces, delivery times vary:

  • **In-Stock Items:** 3-5 business days.
  • **Made-to-Order Pieces:** 4-6 weeks (includes crafting and quality assurance).
  • All shipments require signature confirmation upon delivery for security.

The Aurum Lifetime Warranty

Aurum guarantees the material and craftsmanship of every piece of jewellery for its lifetime against manufacturing defects. This includes loose pave stones, metal discoloration, and structural failures.

The warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, abuse, loss of the center stone, or damage caused by non-Aurum repairs.

30-Day Returns & Exchanges

If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, you may return or exchange unworn merchandise in its original condition within 30 days of shipment. Bespoke or personalized items are final sale. All returns are subject to inspection by our master jewellers.

Privacy Policy

Aurum is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only necessary information for order fulfillment, communication, and personalization of your experience. Your data is never sold to third parties. We use secure, industry-standard encryption for all transactions and data storage.
