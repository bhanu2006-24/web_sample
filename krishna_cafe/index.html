About Krishna Café

Nestled close to the tranquil waters of Lake Sarovar, Krishna Café radiates comfort for students, families, and friends alike. Famous for refreshing mango lassi, flavorful fusion wraps, and the joy of chess nights — Krishna Café is not just a spot for food, it’s a feeling.
